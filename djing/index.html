History of DJing

  • 1943 Jimmy Savile - the first DJ Dance Party
  • 1969 Francis Grasso - started beatmatching
  • 1973 DJ Kool Herc - the father of hip-hop
  • Early 1980s - House music started in Chicago - The Warehouse, Frankie Knuckles, Godfather of House (RIP)
  • Late 1980s - Techno started in Detroit
  • 90s - Acid House, Trance, Raves
